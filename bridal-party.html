Casey Kramer - Maid of Honor

Kristina met Casey Kramer in her freshman year at Duquesne University. The two girls were lucky enough to be on the same floor in their freshman dorm. You often hear that the people you meet during your first year in college will remain your close friends for life, and Kristina is lucky to have met a few! Since then, Casey and Kristina have enjoyed studying abroad, living together in the Southside of Pittsburgh, and many other fun activities!

Angela Bucci

Angela and Kristina met at Duquesne, as they were in the same pharmacy class for six years. Even though they did not discover their friendship until third year, but Kristina considers herself lucky to have had such a great friend and study partner by her side through the years. Kristina and Angela spent most of their time in the library, but when they needed to unwind they enjoyed heading to the Southside and sharing a “fish bowl”. Dana Kessler

Kristina had gone to school with Dana her whole life, although they only began their friendship on the bus freshman year in high school. Luckily, it was early on in high school, because their friendship grew quickly from there and they had a great time together throughout high school. Katie Jones

Katie Jones is one of Kristina's oldest friends. Their friendship began back in 2nd grade when Katie and her family moved across the street. They were the best of friends, playing with the neighborhood kids, having sleepovers, and playing in their creation “Katie's Cafe”. Throughout the years, Katie has moved around the country many times, but Katie and Kristina never ended their friendship, as they were great pen pals. Luckily, Katie kept coming back, having moved to three different homes in Voorhees, N.J., always within a block of Kristina's home. Although they don't see each other as much these days, they will always be lifelong friends.
