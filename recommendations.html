Things to do

  • Visit the Reading Terminal Market. This is a great place to go purchase some local fare, interact with the Amish, or if you're hungry but don't know exactly what you want.
  • Check out the Italian Market. It's the largest and oldest market in the United States, where you'll be sure to see some interesting culture and cuisine.
  • Visit any of the parks. Rittenhouse Square, Washington Square, and Franklin Square are our personal favorites and they're excellent for people watching.
  • You will be in the birthplace of America, so be sure to visit Independence Hall, The Liberty Bell, American Philosophical Society, Benjamin Franklin's house, National Constitution Center, etc.
  • Visit the Philadelphia Art Museum. You might as well run up the steps and throw your hands in the arm when you get to top as well, you'll fit in better this way.
